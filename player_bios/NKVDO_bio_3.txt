Born in 1890. From a peasant family. Joined the Bolsheviks in 1917 and served in the Red Army as political commissar. Transitioned to a career in the OGPU/NKVD, worked your way up to deputy chief position. Good friends with Sergei Sverdlovskii, the Oblast leader.
GOAL: Avoid being sent to the Gulag or Executed.
GOAL: Investigate and arrest any potential threats to the state.
